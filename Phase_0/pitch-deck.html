The Problem

Production is your only chaos lab.

💸

Outages are expensive

Amazon loses ~$220k/min in downtime. Most teams find out what breaks when it breaks.

🧩

Microservices are invisible

No one person understands the full dependency graph. Auth fails → payment fails → orders queue explodes.

🔥

Cascading failures are non-obvious

A 200ms delay in one service becomes a 12-second chain reaction. Retry storms amplify everything.

🛠️

Tools are complex or expensive

Gremlin costs $thousands/mo. Chaos Mesh requires a full K8s cluster. There's no visual simulator.

The Solution

A visual chaos simulator with AI intelligence.
